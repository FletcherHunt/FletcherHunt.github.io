My name is Fletcher Hunt, and I study at ACU I am completing my 3rd year at the university in hopes of becoming a design and technology teacher. I am currently employed at St Marys cathedral college in the city and am employed under the role of LSO and TAS Assistant this job means I am involved with the preparation and running of all technology classrooms and oversee all maintenance to do with the workshops, furthermore I am in charge of stock ordering and project budgeting for both existing and new projects throughout the school year, in addition I work closely with year 12 industrial timber majors on their major work projects giving guidance, reassurance and assistance when needed to ensure they are able to create a major work they can be proud of.
